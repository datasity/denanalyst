Common Questions

FAQs

Do I need experience to apply? +
For Field Researcher and Data Analyst roles — no prior experience required. Full training is provided before each project. For Research Lead roles we ask for at least one prior research or data project you can reference.
Is this paid work? +
Current projects are voluntary and portfolio-based. Contributors receive published research credit, a public profile, and mentorship. As the platform grows and projects attract funding, we aim to introduce stipends for field work.
How much time does it take? +
Field researchers typically commit 1–2 days per week during data collection phases (6–10 weeks). Analysts and writers work more flexibly — usually 3–5 hours per week, fully remote.
Do I need to be based in Nigeria? +
Field researchers need to be local to the study area. Data analysts, writers, and research leads can contribute remotely from anywhere in the world.
How long does the review process take? +
We review all applications within 5 working days and reply with next steps. If a project isn't currently accepting contributors for your role, we'll put you on the waitlist and notify you when one opens.
Will I get a profile page on the site? +
Yes — once you've been accepted and contributed to at least one project, we create a public contributor profile page for you on the site showing your projects, role, and skills.
